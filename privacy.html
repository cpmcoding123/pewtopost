Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

At PewToPost, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, church name, and phone number when you submit sermons
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store your credit card details)
  • Sermon Content: Video files, scripture references, and any special instructions you provide
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your sermon submissions and create social media clips
  • Manage your subscription and process payments
  • Communicate with you about your orders and our services
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and website functionality
  • Send important updates about our service (with your permission)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Stripe for payment processing, Google for form submissions and file storage
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Your Sermon Content

Your sermon videos and created clips are your intellectual property. We:

  • Only use your content to provide our services
  • Do not share your content with third parties without your permission
  • Store your content securely using industry-standard practices
  • Will delete your content upon request after service completion

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at pewtopost@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Sermon content is typically retained for 30 days after processing unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for churches and organizations, not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Services We Use:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Google Forms/Drive: Form submissions and file storage (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel: Website hosting (Vercel Privacy Policy)

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. International Users

If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: pewtopost@gmail.com
  • Website: pewtopost.vercel.app

By using PewToPost, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
